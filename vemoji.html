Vemoji

What if the interface of the connected car uses emotive power of eye expressions?

Connected cars provide a sense of security on the road, but the interface of these technologies is passive and trapped in an emergency button experience metaphor.


What if sense of safety could be conveyed via continues, discrete sense of ambient emotional  presence. As cars become “smarter” we can reimagine new forms of communication, new forms of relationship with our cars.

Vemoji is a driving companion, emotional engine, continuously linking us to the intelligence of connected cars. Vemoji translates essential connected cars' information through a palette of empathetic eye cues. These emotive cues provide an instant and continuous connection between human and the car.
