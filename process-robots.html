What if we reimagine what the connection between technology and our world could be?

Room For More Possibilities is about unlocking the inventive potential of our relationship with technology, to inspire new emotional links between people and brands. Room for More Possibilities is an Invention Design process and talent unit.

Brands are more than stories. Brands are invention platforms. Inventions are new doors to brand stories.
The Biggest cultural advantage we can give a brand is the future. Inventions make future happen faster.

Our process is about asking “what if?" Investigating, experimenting, and even “dreaming" in order to encourage thinking about how things could be, not just how they are. We think about the kind of world we would like to live in.
We need to speculate to disrupt the present, imagine how things could be and make room for more possibilities.
Invention Design process is fostered through new roles:
Invention strategist/ invention designer/ poetic technologist.

We design inventions* to expand possibilities how brand stories are told and experienced.
*Invention today is not about making life more automated but about inspiring new behaviors and new forms of inhabiting technology with our humanity.
We identify possibilities of new emotional links between people and brands.
We invent new forms of ‘story-making’.
We develop brands' invention platforms.
We develop invention strategies and proposals.
We design invention prototypes.Invention proposal organize design investigation through four key elements:
WHY? – Identifies an opportunity for cultural intervention through the brand.
WHAT IF? – Invites new human goals and values.
CONTEXT? - Investigates human tactics and tools of cultural disobedience.
INVENTION METAPHOR: Activates power of objects to invite new behaviors and sensibilities to inspire new emotional links between people and the brand. Informs Invention Platform.

Invention Strategy sees the future as a possibility, not just a problem to be solved. It translates the brand mission and the vision of the future into tangible objects, products, and experiences that inspire new emotional links between people and brands.

Invention Design imagines new form factors and interactions. Invention Design is an agile process that rapidly prototypes the future, inviting us into more alternatives and possibilities for the world we live in.

We take pictures of every most mundane moment of our lives but everything ends up in an infinite digital space of forgetting. Unlimited and effortless quantity is at odds with the need to mark moments that are truly special.

Sound Capsule inspires new yet simple human gesture: sound catching. It invites us to recognize in a new way moments, atmosphere, and mark them as special. Sound Capsule is an analog object that uses poetic potential of technology and evocative power of sound. With its one time recording function, Sound Capsule helps us mark unique moments and rescue them from digital obsolescence.
