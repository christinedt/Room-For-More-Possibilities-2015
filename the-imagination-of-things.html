The Imagination of Things

How can people re-discover the power of Lego bricks to reimagine how the physical and virtual can come together?

When everything becomes connected, bricks can be a tool to re-imagine how the virtual and the physical can come together. Welcome to the imagination of things!

Lego Heart

Lego creates a special brick called “Lego Heart,” as a symbol of how emotional our relationship with technology become. Lego Heart is a simple brick with a glowing heart and haptic feedback that can give life to any LEGO creation: from a wearable to a robot.

Sound Brick

When the digital and the physical world meets, it can bring life to any Lego project. Each sound brick works as a tiny interactive speaker and is connected with your device (iPad/iPhone/PC) so the sound can be customized. Bricks can talk to each other, creating an interactive soundscape. Sounds are triggered when you come in close proximity to a sound brick. Sound Brick can also be programmed to play when it is touched or at time- based intervals.
