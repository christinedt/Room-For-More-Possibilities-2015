Slice
Sourcing

What if a loyalty program could be powered by collaboration?



1/ Join the slice sourcing community.




2/ Accrue slices.



3/ Trade slices to complete and share full pie.

Loyalty programs are often quite “selfish” and at odds with the Shared Economy we live in. They are automatic, using abstract reward points as currency of loyalty.


"Pizza Slice Sourcing" is a collaborative reward system with social benefits that allows people to receive their free pizza faster, with a little help from others.There are no points, only slices, pies and relationships that make pizza happen faster. Slices are the unit of exchange that makes loyalty a collaborative experience.
