Invention Strategy sees the future as a possibility, not just a problem to be solved.



Invention Strategy translates the brand mission and vision of the future into tangible objects, products and experiences to inspire new emotional links between people and brands.

Invention Strategist

The Invention Strategist investigates curious states of our relationship with technology through deep empathy towards human goals and values.Invention strategy challenges narrow assumptions about the role products, brands and technology plays in our lives to identify those opportunities for cultural intervention for the brand.

Invention Designer

The Invention Designer probes opportunities for new forms of storymaking through the exploration of new form factors, capable of inspiring new behaviors, and engaging our imaginative collaboration. The Invention Designer is a rapid prototyper that can turn invention proposals into physical objects and experiences through sketches, storyboards and models, driving the development of invention proposal with imagination and ingenuity.

Poetic Technologist

The Poetic Technologist’s role is to re-imagine what the connection between technology and our world could be, merging the virtual and the physical in new ways. Poetic Technology goes beyond technological trends and proposes another vision of the future of our relationship with technology through tangible prototyping and imaginative creative technology skills.
